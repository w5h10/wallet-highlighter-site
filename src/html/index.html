Why do I need Wallet Highlighter?
Wallet Highlighter offers proactive protection by identifying high-risk wallets, helping you make informed decisions and trade with confidence.
What is Risk score?
Wallet Highlighter assigns a risk score to wallet addresses based on their historical participation with threat actors. This score serves as a valuable indicator of the potential risk associated with a particular wallet. A higher risk score suggests a higher likelihood of the wallet being linked to scams, phishing, or other fraudulent activities. By considering the risk score, you can make more informed decisions and navigate the crypto landscape with confidence.
